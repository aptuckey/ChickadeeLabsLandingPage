Delete Your Account

Follow these steps to permanently delete your Roadside App account.

  1. Open the Roadside app on your device.
  2. Sign in to your account if you aren't already logged in.
  3. Select the "You" tab from the bottom menu bar.
  4. Tap the gear icon (⚙️) located in the upper-right corner to open Settings.
  5. Scroll through the settings menu to find and tap the "Delete Account" option.
  6. A confirmation dialog will appear. Please read it carefully and tap "Confirm" to proceed with the deletion.

Once this final step is complete, your account and all associated data will be deleted immediately and cannot be recovered.
