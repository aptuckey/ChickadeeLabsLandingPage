Our Mission

Promoting Healthier Screen Habits

Our goal for the app was to help users follow the 20-20-20 rule. 20-20-20 is a guideline that helps reduce eye strain. Every 20 minutes, look away from your screen and focus on an object that is 20 feet away for at least 20 seconds.
